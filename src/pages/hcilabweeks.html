Interactive installation

I explored Human-Computer Interaction Technologies with a focus on technology as the starting point. I researched emerging technologies like Playful Interaction, Extended Reality, and Artificial Creatures, and learned to identify new opportunities for interactions. Through the course, I developed an innovative interactive concept using tools such as sensors and Arduino, which broadened my understanding of interaction design and allowed for creative expression.

Playful interactions

Over 7 intense weeks, I learned to use Arduino by tackling various challenges. In the final 2 weeks, we spent 10 days in a designated space in The Hague, where we applied everything we had learned. This hands-on experience allowed us to put our skills into practice. Without further ado, here is my team's playful interaction project, presented as a video.

I'm an Estonian living in the Netherlands, and I just finished my degree in UX design with distinction.🎓 What I love most about UX design is the design thinking process specifically research and analysis phases, as well as creating wireframes. I like digging into the details of designs, figuring out what works well and what doesn't, and finding solutions to problems. If I had to choose, then expert review and accessibility audit would be my favorite research methods.

When I'm not working, I love trying out new things and experiencing different hobbies, including reading 📖, baking🧁, and playing on Switch 🎮. I am also always looking for new challenges to help me grow, whether that means running 20km in the mud or going waterskiing.🧭
